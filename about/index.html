About VEEL

Built on craft.
Driven by results.

VEEL Consulting is a boutique digital agency specialising in web design and Salesforce consulting for ambitious SMEs. We exist to close the gap between enterprise-quality digital work and the businesses that need it most.

The Story

Why VEEL exists

VEEL began as an idea in 2018 — the observation that small and medium businesses deserve the same quality of digital work as the largest enterprises, but rarely get it. Too often they're handed templates, generic CRM setups, and websites that were never built to perform.

The name VEEL is a pure brand. No acronym, no explanation needed. It stands for the standard we hold our work to: uncommon quality in everything we produce.

What we believe

We believe that digital infrastructure is a lever — not a cost. A well-built website compounds. A properly configured Salesforce org removes friction from your most important processes every single day. Done right, this work pays for itself many times over.

We also believe in partnership over transactions. Our best client relationships are long ones, built on trust that accumulates over time as we learn your business and you trust our craft.

Who we work with

Our clients are SMEs with ambition. Founders who know their product is exceptional and want their digital presence to match. Sales leaders frustrated by CRM chaos. Marketing teams who know they're leaving conversions on the table. We work best with people who care about doing things properly.
